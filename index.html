About


Mark Elliot Zuckerberg was born in May 14, 1984. Is an American technology entrepreneur and philanthropist. He is known for co-founding and leading Facebook as its chairman and chief executive officer.



"When you give everyone a voice and give people power, the system usually ends up in a really good place. So, what we view our role as, is giving people that power".
